PPSA Physician Survey

In a recent Kyruus suvey, over 95% of physicians responded they were concerned about errors in publicly reported industry interaction data. The implications for life science companies and hospitals are substantial.

  • 78% of physicians want hospital administrators to play an active role in identifying and managing errors.
  • Almost 50% of respondents would inform peers of inaccuracies.
  • Over 50% of physicians would reduce interactions as a result of the error.
